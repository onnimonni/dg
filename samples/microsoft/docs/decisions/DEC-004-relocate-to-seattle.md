# Relocate from Albuquerque to Seattle

## Setting
Microsoft is growing, but recruiting top talent to Albuquerque, New Mexico is difficult. Bill and Paul are both from Seattle.

## People
- **Responsible**: Bill Gates, Paul Allen
- **Approvers**: Bill Gates
- **Consulted**: Employees
- **Informed**:

## Alternatives

### Option A: Stay in Albuquerque
**Pros:**
- Low cost of living.
- Less distraction.

**Cons:**
- Hard to recruit engineers.
- Travel is difficult (flight connections).

### Option B: Move to Bay Area (Silicon Valley)
**Pros:**
- Center of the tech world.
- Huge talent pool.

**Cons:**
- High competition for talent.
- Expensive.
- Distracting.

### Option C: Move to Seattle (Bellevue)
**Pros:**
- Home turf for Bill and Paul.
- Good talent pool (University of Washington, Boeing).
- Quality of life.
- No state income tax.

**Cons:**
- Not yet a tech hub.
- Rain.

## Decision
Chosen: **Option C**

Rationale: Better recruiting, personal preference (home), and establishing a unique culture away from the Valley echo chamber.

## Consequences

### Positive
- Massive recruiting success.
- Created the Seattle tech ecosystem.
